“R” - Rosé First Single Album


R (also stylized as -R-) is the debut single album by Rosé. It was released on March 12, 2021, with "On The Ground" serving as the album's title track. The single album holds a much more mature appearance and music, is a concept of a retro/cinematic mood, and has more value of obtaining since Rosé participated in the album design herself.
